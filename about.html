Who We Are

ALIF Skin Care is a trusted Pakistani skincare brand focused on safe, effective and beginner-friendly products. We believe that everyone deserves healthy, glowing skin without complicated routines.

Our Ingredients Philosophy

We use well-researched active ingredients backed by science:

  • Niacinamide – reduces acne, controls oil, improves barrier
  • Vitamin C – brightens, fades pigmentation, boosts glow
  • Glycolic Acid – exfoliates and smooths uneven texture
  • Salicylic Acid – clears pores and prevents breakouts

These ingredients work together to improve overall skin tone, reduce dark spots, and enhance natural radiance.

Designed For All Skin Types

Whether you have oily, dry, combination, or sensitive skin, our products minimize irritation and deliver results gradually and safely.

Every formula is created to be gentle yet effective, especially for beginners who are new to active ingredients.
